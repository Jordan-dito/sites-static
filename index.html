About Us

Who we are?

We work closely with our fully vetted and approved national providers. WitFolk can help you to safely move around within areas of low, medium or high risk. With our network of trusted security personnel, we offer a broad range of capabilities from simple airport "meet and greets" to full scale tracking services as a swift professional assistance exactly when you need us.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver reliable protection and risk assessment strategies, ensuring safety and trust for our clients worldwide.

Vision

Efficiently and effectively achieve the results proposed by the organization, combine business development with social prosperity, and even go beyond what was planned.

Scope

WitFolk offer a full range of security services for corporates, high & low profile and private individuals. We provide protection for individuals and assets, security coverage at any corporate facility or private residence and travel risk management to mitigate risk for private individuals or corporates operating within complex environments. Logistical support is provided in all cases enabling one secure point of coordination with highly trained, knowledgeable and trusted personnel who will drive the most appropriate vehicles safely to the final destination.

Our experience

We have undertaken innumerable international assignments involving multi-destination visits. We understand the need for flexibility, reliability and consistency in service delivery.

Our core values

  • Customer Focus

Heat

Hostile Environment Awareness Training

Three to five days of intensive training in a real-life exercise. Training teaches skills for navigating dangerous situations aiming to provide staff members and employees how they can minimize their risk and protect themselves through a personal security and awareness plan.

Training Modules

Personal Security Awareness

Being aware of surroundings and taking proactive measures.

Travel Safety

Pre-travel preparation and emergency situations.

Emergency Management

Preparing for and responding to security issues.

Basic Negotiations Skills

Strategic management tools with conflict-sensitive approach.

Weapon Familiarization

Classroom instruction and hands-on practice.

Gender-Based Violence (GBV)

Education on preventing and responding to GBV.

Core Survival Skills

Essential skills like shelters, water, fire, and first aid.

Purpose

To teach individuals how to stay safe when working in high-risk areas.

Participants learn behavioral changes and practical skills to respond to security risks.
